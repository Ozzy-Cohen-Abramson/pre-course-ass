About

My name is Oz Cohen Abramson. I got to the world of coding because I felt any other job I had wasn't challenging enough. Furthermore, I love to learn new things and as a developer this is an essential quality.

I did my B.Ed degree in Science (biology , chimestry and phisics) at Herzog college.
My M.A. is in science and jewish law (mainly medicine and ethics).

After I finished my B.Ed degree I started teaching. After 3 years I came to the conclusion that I'm not fully applying my abilities and started to learn coding by myself.
During my spare time I do music and play guitar in a band.

I was born in 1994 in Hadera. At age 18 I moved to Jerusalem to study and to serve in the military. At the age of 25 I moved back to Hadera.
In the future I would like to visit Dublin and Los Angeles.
